About Shiwft Express

Shiwft Express is a platform for VIT students to help each other with pickups and drops. Whether it's a courier, a book, or anything else, post your request and let others help you out!

Our Mission

To make campus life easier by connecting students who need help with those who can provide it.

Community Driven

Built by students, for students. We believe in the power of community.

Safe & Secure

Verified VIT student emails ensure a safe and trustworthy environment.
